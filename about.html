About Us

Helping people find their lost belongings with ease.

Who We Are

Lost & Found is a platform dedicated to helping people reunite with their lost items. Whether it's a misplaced phone, a lost wallet, or any valuable possession, we make it easy to report and search for lost and found items.

Our Mission

We aim to create a community-driven space where people can post about lost items, report found belongings, and help each other recover valuable possessions efficiently.

How It Works

  • Report a lost item with details and images.
  • Report a found item to help reunite it with its owner.
  • Search through reported items to find your lost belongings.
